While this isn't necessarily a public re-useable project, you can certainly clone it, wipe out all of my content, and then use it for your own!

This project is a record of all the books that I have read, at least going back to when I got my first Kindle. I started out by scraping my library borrowing history and purchases from Amazon, and now I add books to it as I check them out from the library.
